Summer Sunflowers

Bring a touch of sunshine to your dessert table with our Sunflower Vanilla Cupcakes. These delightful treats are crafted from moist, fluffy vanilla cake and artfully decorated to resemble blooming sunflowers. Each cupcake is topped with rich, creamy yellow frosting, and finished with decadent chocolate chunks in the center to mimic sunflower seeds. Perfect for any garden party, summer celebration, or simply to brighten someone's day.

Enjoy the taste of summer with every bite!
